Vortex Gallery Online is a yearly tournament series, this time spanning 4 weekends across 2 months. Once again, we're partnering with communities all around the word for an online event where we can come together & play the underrepresented games we all love!

**Join us in one of our 100+ tournaments we'll be running starting October 11th!**

### Vortex Gallery Online 2024 Schedule
Vortex Gallery Online 2024 will be taking place across 4 weekends in 2 *chapters* this year:
- **Chapter 1:** 10/11-13, 10/18-20
- **Chapter 2:** 11/01-03, 11/08-10

Join us on [Discord](https://discord.gg/QDU8kEyDXM) and [Twitter](https://twitter.com/956productions) to stay tuned. **This is #VGON24.**
